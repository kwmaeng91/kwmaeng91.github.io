Research Interest

I am generally interested in designing systems (compilers, languages, hardware, software, ...) for resource-constrained embedded devices.

  • Improving Programmability of Embedded Devices
    Embedded system programming is frustrating: platforms are fragmented, physical properties must be considered, many new security vulnerabilities exist, resources are limited and heterogeneous, and you always need to go through piles of datasheets. I am interested in the intersection of PL and embedded systems to make programming simpler for embedded devices, especially for those with fragmented and heterogeneous hardware.

  • Tiny Systems and ML
    I am also interested in exploring the interplay between tiny embedded devices and machine learning (ML). Broadly, I aim to seek answers for the following questions:
    • How can we run ML training/inference efficiently on tiny systems?
    • How can ML help designing efficient HW/SW for tiny systems?

  • Batteryless, Energy-harvesting Systems
    I have been working on various aspects of designing batteryless embedded devices that use energy from the environment to operate. Please see my publications for more.
